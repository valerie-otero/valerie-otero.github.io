Features

Built for pilot briefings

Worldwide data

Direct access to NOAA servers for real-time METAR and TAF, wherever you are.

Visual decoding

Instantly see flight conditions (VFR, MVFR, IFR, LIFR) thanks to a clear color code.

Route management

Create airport groups, organize your flights and view weather across the whole route.

Sunrise / sunset and civil twilight calculated from your position and applicable rules.
